01. ABOUT

I am a senior broker-dealer executive with 25 years of experience in diverse capacities, having served as Chief Executive Officer, Chief Operating Officer, and Chief Compliance Officer for multiple firms, as well as working extensively as a consultant and former regulator over the last 24 years. Throughout my career, I have encountered a broad range of operational and regulatory challenges and have made it a professional priority to develop clear, effective strategies to address them.

In addition to my corporate and consulting roles, I have served on nonprofit boards and a public company board. These engagements have reinforced my belief that leadership extends beyond the balance sheet, requiring a dedication to stewardship, ethical decision-making, and the betterment of the broader community. Currently based in Johns Creek, Georgia, I look forward to hearing from you.
